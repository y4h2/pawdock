# Naming Design

## Decision

- **Application name**: Pawdock (Paw + Dock, a little paw docked on your desktop)
- **Character name**: Nuonuo / 糯糯 (soft, sticky, cute — like mochi)

## Rationale

### Pawdock

- International-friendly, easy to spell and remember
- Combines "paw" (animal companion) with "dock" (desktop presence)
- Works as a brand name, distinct from the character identity

### Nuonuo / 糯糯

- Doubled character (叠字) naturally sounds cute in Chinese
- 糯 evokes soft, warm, chewy texture — matches the character's fluffy fur-trimmed design and warm beige/brown color palette
- Romanized "Nuonuo" is pronounceable across languages

## Character Profile

- Appearance: chibi girl with animal ears, fluffy tail, small crown, fur-trimmed cloak
- Color palette: warm beige, brown, cream
- Personality: soft, cuddly, loyal companion
